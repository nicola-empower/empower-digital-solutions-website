"Digital Transformation" is a buzzword. "Digital Engine" is a blueprint.

At Empower Digital Solutions, we don't just design pages; we engineer flows. But what does that actually look like for a service business?

## The 3-Stage Engine

### 1. The Intake (Fuel)
Your website needs to be a magnet, not a billboard.
*   **High-Value Lead Magnets:** ROI Calculators, Free Audits, or "Instant Quotes".
*   **Frictionless Forms:** Smart forms that adapt based on user answers.

### 2. The Processor (Engine)
What happens *after* they click submit?
*   **Instant CRM Sync:** Leads go straight into your CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive, etc.).
*   **Auto-Qualification:** If they don't meet your budget, send a polite "referral" email. If they do, send a calendar link.

### 3. The Output (Motion)
*   **Automated Follow-ups:** "Nurture sequences" that educate the client while you sleep.
*   **Client Portal Access:** Once signed, they get instant access to a project dashboard.
