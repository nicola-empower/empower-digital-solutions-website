## The "Price Anchor"

Your website is the first interaction a client has with your brand. It sets the "Price Anchor."

*   **Generic Template:** Signals "Commodity." Clients will haggle.
*   **Bespoke Experience:** Signals "Expert." Clients expect to pay more.

## Case Study: Cairn Architects

When we built the site for *Cairn Architects*, we didn't just show pictures of houses. We built an immersive experience. We used motion, whitespace, and typography to mirror the quality of their physical work.

The result? They attracted higher-budget clients who valued design, not just square footage.
